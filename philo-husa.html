Heart of USA

Recognizing, supporting, and giving back to the farmworkers who keep our communities fed.

Our Story Impact Scholarship Support

Our Story

Started in 2020. Built on gratitude.

Heart of USA began in 2020 to recognize and support farmworkers who continued working during the COVID-19 pandemic and are often overlooked. While many could work from home, farmworkers kept showing up—helping keep food on tables across the country.

Each year we bring resources, appreciation, and community to the fields—partnering with local organizations and volunteers to provide food, supplies, and support.

How we show up

  • Resource tables and essentials
  • Meals and refreshments
  • Raffles, gifts, and appreciation
  • Community partnerships and outreach

Why Farmworkers Matter

Central Valley work supports national food security.

  • The Central Valley produces a major share of the nation’s food supply.
  • Agriculture is a cornerstone of California’s economy.
  • Much of the work is labor-intensive and not easily replaced.
  • Supporting farmworkers supports stable access to food for everyone.

Heart of USA Scholarship

Supporting first-generation students from farmworking backgrounds.

Eligibility

  • Incoming first-generation freshman
  • Applicant or family from a farmworking background
  • Financial need statement
  • Good academic standing

Application includes

  • Online application
  • Most recent high school transcript
  • ~500-word personal statement (career goals + what first-gen means to you)
  • Proof of university acceptance
