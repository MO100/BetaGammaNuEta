Policies

Risk Management & Code of Conduct

Our commitment to safety, respect, and accountability.

Alcohol & Drugs Hazing Sexual Misconduct Assault Firearms Retaliation Distribution

Alcohol and Drug Policy

Purpose and Scope

In compliance with Federal, State, and University requirements, Beta Gamma Nu is committed to a drug-free environment where students can pursue higher education free of illegal drugs and alcohol abuse...

Rules of Compliance

  1. Members and guests must comply with all federal, state, and local laws...
  2. No possession, use, or sale of illegal drugs or controlled substances...
  3. Alcohol only via licensed vendor or BYOB; no beverages above 15% ABV...
  4. No bulk/common sources of alcohol...
  5. No fraternity funds may purchase alcohol...
  6. No co-sponsoring events with groups that provide alcohol or drugs...
  7. No co-sponsoring with bars or promoters...
  8. Guest list required; 3:1 guest-to-member ratio...
  9. New member events must be substance-free...
  10. No drinking games or rapid consumption activities...
  11. No drinking while wearing fraternity letters in public/social media...

Hazing Policy

Definition: Hazing includes any act, whether on or off fraternity property, intended to recruit, initiate, or retain membership that causes physical, mental, or emotional harm.

  • Coercion to break laws or policies.
  • Forced consumption of food, drink, alcohol, or drugs.
  • Physical brutality: paddling, branding, exposure to elements.
  • Mental brutality: sleep deprivation, humiliation, exclusion.
  • Any conduct harming health, safety, or academics.
  • Members involved will face judicial review and possible termination.

Sexual Misconduct Policy

Purpose and Scope

We uphold a zero-tolerance policy for sexual misconduct in any form...

Rules of Compliance

  1. Affirmative consent is required for all sexual activity.
  2. No demeaning or sexually abusive events or activities.
  3. All members are obligated to report sexual misconduct.
  4. No use of strippers or similar entertainment at fraternity events.

Assault & Battery Policy

Beta Gamma Nu is committed to a violence-free environment...

  1. No member or guest may engage in assault or battery at any fraternity event.
  2. Members found guilty will have membership status reviewed or revoked.
  3. All incidents must be reported to the National Board within 24 hours and university authorities within 72 hours.

Firearms, Explosives & Incendiary Devices

Possession or use of firearms, explosives, or incendiary devices on fraternity property or events is strictly prohibited, unless in full compliance with all applicable laws and never in a fraternity capacity.

Retaliation Policy

No brother may retaliate against another member or non-member for reporting violations of these policies. Whistleblowers are protected.

Distribution Policy

All new members receive a copy of the Risk Management Policy at their first official meeting. Updates are redistributed to the entire membership and always available on the Members-Only portal on the official website.
